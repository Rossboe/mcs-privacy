Privacy Policy

MCS — Music Creation Station · Last updated: 20 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MCS — Music Creation Station ("MCS", "the app", "we", "us") handles information. MCS is a music-creation app for Android developed by Rossboe781.

MCS is built to keep your work on your device. There is no account or login, and we do not collect personal information about you for our own use.

Information MCS handles

Audio you record (microphone). MCS lets you record short audio clips with your device's microphone to use as samples in your music. This audio is processed and stored entirely on your device. It is never uploaded to us or to any third party, and we have no access to it. Deleting a sample in the app, or uninstalling the app, removes it.

Your projects, samples, and settings. Beats, patterns, songs, imported sounds, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We cannot access this content. Uninstalling the app deletes it.

Purchases. MCS offers an optional one-time "Pro" in-app purchase. Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing and managed through RevenueCat, our purchase-management provider. To deliver and restore your purchase, these services process information such as your purchase and transaction details, a randomly generated app-user identifier, and basic device information (for example, device type, operating-system version, and country). We do not receive or store your name, email address, or payment-card details — payment is handled entirely by Google Play.

Microphone permission. MCS requests microphone access only for the in-app sampler. It is used only while you are actively recording, and only for the on-device purpose described above.

Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or contacts.
  • We do not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • We do not include third-party advertising.
  • We do not include third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for advertising.

How information is used

The limited purchase information described above is used solely to:

  • process and validate your in-app purchase,
  • unlock and restore your "Pro" features across reinstalls and devices, and
  • detect and prevent fraudulent or invalid purchases.

Third-party services

MCS relies on the following services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Billing (payments) — https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat (purchase management) — https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Data retention and deletion

  • On-device data (projects, samples, recordings, settings) is removed when you delete it in the app or uninstall MCS.
  • Purchase records held by Google and RevenueCat are retained under their policies and as required for accounting and to support purchase restoration. To request deletion of purchase data associated with your device, contact us at the email below and we will assist.

Children's privacy

MCS is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Your creative content stays on your device. Purchase processing is handled by established providers (Google, RevenueCat) over encrypted connections. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example, the EEA/UK under the GDPR, or California under the CCPA), you may have rights to access or delete personal data. Because MCS keeps your content on your device and does not hold an identity-linked profile, most data is directly in your control. For purchase-related data held by our providers, contact us and we will help.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for significant changes, note it in the app or store listing.
