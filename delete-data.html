Data Deletion Request

MCS — Music Creation Station · Developer: Rossboe781 · Last updated: 20 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of data related to MCS — Music Creation Station, the Android app published by Rossboe781. MCS has no account or login and keeps your creative work on your device, so most data is already under your direct control. See also our Privacy Policy.

How to request deletion

  1. Email us at ross.king781@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line MCS data deletion request.
  3. If you purchased MCS Pro, include your approximate purchase date and the Google Play country/region so we can locate the purchase record. Do not send any payment-card details — we don't need them and never collect them.
  4. We will confirm and action your request within 30 days.

Delete your data yourself — instantly

Your beats, patterns, songs, imported sounds, microphone recordings, and settings are stored only on your device; we never receive them. You can remove them yourself at any time, with no request to us:

  • Delete an individual project or sample inside the app, or
  • Uninstall MCS — this removes all on-device content immediately.

What is deleted and what is kept

Data typeWhat happensRetention
On-device content (projects, samples, microphone recordings, settings) Deleted by you in the app or on uninstall. Not held by us. Removed immediately on your device.
Purchase data held by our purchase provider RevenueCat (purchase history and a randomly generated app-user identifier) Deleted on request using the steps above. Removed within 30 days of your request, except where we must keep limited records to meet legal, tax, or accounting obligations.
Google Play transaction records (handled by Google, not by the app) Managed by Google Play. Request via your Google account. Retained by Google under its own policies and as required by law.
